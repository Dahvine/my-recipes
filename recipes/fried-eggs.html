Fried-eggs

Description

An easy, fast and delicious recipe for your favourite scrambled egg

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Skillet
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Spray small non-stick skillet with cooking spray. Heat skillet over medium heat.
  2. Break eggs into skillet. Cook until done as desired.
  3. Transfer eggs to plate, yolk up. Season with salt and pepper.
